Beach day

 

The O'Brien family are spending the day at the beach. The want to find the perfect umbrella. The umbrellas are numbered 1-10.

First, they need to be sure the umbrella is free. It is free, if both sunbeds don’t have towels on them.

Second, they don’t want to be directly next to where people walk. They can check for footprints in the sand.

Third, they don’t want to be directly next to any wet sand. They can tell if the sand is wet by itsdarker colour.

Fourth, they don’t want to sit where they will hear music coming from the people next to them.

The range of the music is marked on in the pictures with music notes in the squar

Finally, they want to have some space to play in the sand, at least 2 squares should be enough space.
