School visits/workshops

For primary and secondary school teachers, we offer free online workshops. We can also hold an in-person teacher workshop after a computational thinking school visit (see below).
Workshops
Our workshops help teachers integrate computational thinking activities (based on Bebras tasks) into their classrooms. No prior experience of computational thinking is necessary. The next available workshops are listed below. In addition, we can run a two-/three-hour online workshop at any mutually agreeable time if four or more teachers from a school get together to contact us. All workshops are free.
    Our next workshops are planned for September 2023.
Teachers
If you are a teacher and have any query about workshops please email us atpact@mu.ie.
School visits
We offer FREE half-day and day-long on-site school visits, funded through Science Foundation Ireland. Our visits include running Computational Thinking Obstacle Courses for different age groups and CT-teacher training for teaching staff. Visits are offered on a first-come-first-served basis, so contact us atpact@mu.ieas soon as your school allows.
